# 🏰 Wizarding World Explorer ✨⚡

An immersive and interactive fan-made web application inspired by the magical world of Harry Potter.

Wizarding World Explorer allows fans to dive deep into characters, spells, magical creatures, hidden lore, book-only facts, mini games, and secrets that were never shown in the movies.

## 🌟 Features

### 🧙 Character Encyclopedia

* Explore major and minor characters.
* Learn about houses, wands, patronuses, families, and relationships.
* Discover hidden character details and book-only information.

### 📚 Things The Movies Never Told You

* Book-only facts and hidden lore.
* Missing storylines and removed scenes.
* Lesser-known details from the wizarding world.

### 🪄 Spell Book

* Spell names and pronunciations.
* Meanings and uses.
* Characters who used them.
* Interesting trivia and facts.

### 🐉 Magical Creatures

* Discover creatures from the wizarding world.
* Learn their abilities, habitats, and histories.

### 🏰 Magical Locations

* Explore important locations and their secrets.
* Interactive pages for famous places.

### 🗺️ Interactive Hogwarts Map

* Click locations to reveal facts and hidden details.

### 🎮 Mini Games

* Potion Brewing Game
* Spell Casting Challenge
* Wizard Quiz
* Hidden Object Hunt
* Trivia Challenge

### 🏆 Achievements and Collectibles

* Unlock badges and achievements.
* Collect magical items and secrets.

### 🔍 Search Functionality

* Search characters, spells, creatures, locations, and facts instantly.

### ✨ Magical Experience

* Floating candles
* Magical particles
* Smooth animations
* Glowing effects
* Responsive design
* Beautiful user interface
